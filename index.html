About BSTE

The Board of Science & Technology Education (BSTE) is Pakistan's authoritative body for standardizing technical and scientific curricula, issuing nationally recognized credentials, and accrediting vocational training institutes across the country.

Our Vision

BSTE envisions a future where standardized, fair, and transparent technical assessments drive excellence in vocational education, ensuring equitable opportunities for all learners and institutions across Pakistan.

Our Mission

BSTE ensures standardization and authentication of technical qualifications while upholding assessment integrity through modern methodologies, strengthening national and international collaborations in technical education.

Academic Architecture

Curriculum Frameworks Engineered for
International Industrial Standards

Curriculum frameworks engineered to meet international industrial certification standards — each field aligned with global competency benchmarks and sector-specific skill matrices.

Civil

Construction & Civil Engineering

Structural drafting, surveying, road laboratory, construction site management, building inspection, and AutoCAD-based design — preparing graduates for infrastructure development roles.

Electrical

Electrical & Electronics Technology

Industrial electrician, electronics engineering, CCTV systems, electro-mechanical engineering, and heavy equipment auto-electrics for energy and manufacturing sectors.

IT / Computing

Information Technology & Computing

CCNA, CCNP, CCIE networking certifications, software engineering, database management, web engineering, and freelancing diplomas for Pakistan's growing digital economy.

Telecom

Telecommunications & Networking

Optical fiber systems, telecom technician certification, wireless technology, and system engineering — aligned with Pakistan's telecom sector growth roadmap.

Mechanical

Mechanical & Automotive Engineering

Auto mechanic (diesel), auto electrician, automobile engineering, welding technology, and production engineering for workshop, manufacturing, and automotive industry careers.

HSE

Health, Safety & Environment

Safety officer, inspector and supervisor certifications, fire management, environmental engineering, and sanitation inspection — meeting OSHA-aligned national standards.

Media

Media, Communication & Journalism

Mass communication, TV news reporting, video production, public relations, media literacy, and journalism management — for Pakistan's evolving media landscape.

International

International Relations & Law

Diplomas in foreign policy, international law, diplomacy, international trading, and Pakistan foreign relations — for government, NGO, and diplomatic sector entry.

Creative

Fashion, Design & Creative Arts

Fashion designing, interior decoration, dress making, furniture & wood working, and professional photography — supporting Pakistan's textile and creative industries.

Energy

Energy, Oil & Gas Technology

Petroleum engineering, oil & gas technology, solar energy, HVAC maintenance, and refrigeration & air conditioning — aligned with Pakistan's expanding energy sector.

Healthcare

Healthcare & Medical Technology

Medical equipment technician, radiology technician, pharmaceutical chemistry, and Montessori education diplomas — for Pakistan's growing paramedical sector.

Business

Business, Management & Social Development

Project planning, ERP/material management, transport management, hospitality, social mobilization, and business communication — developing professional managers.
